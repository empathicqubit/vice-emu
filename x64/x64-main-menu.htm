Status Bar

The area at the bottom of the x64 window shows various messages during the operation of the emulator such as when attaching or detaching tape/disk/cartriddge images.

Also making it's home here are other informational items and controls:

  • Disk drive information: Drive number followed by the current track, sector and activity indicator.
  • Joystick display: Shows the current positions of the joystick.
  • Volume control: Slides up and down to control the volume of the emulated machine.
